Facts

Size

They are the largest bony fish - the biggest one ever weighed being over 2.7 tons, growing to over 3m long and 4m from fin to fin!

Life

Sunfish lay more eggs than any other kind of animal - in fact, a single Mola mola can lay over 300 million eggs at a time!

Status

Their population is considered vulnerable. This is due to the fact that Sunfish frequently get snagged in drift gill nets and can suffocate on sea trash, like plastic bags, which resemble jellyfish.
